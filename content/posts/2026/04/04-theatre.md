# Theatre

Last year, or the year before, IDK, I had the idea of a swarm of autonomous agents that would live in my [Goldentooth](https://clog.goldentooth.net) minilab. I didn't end up actually getting very far with the implementation, but I was far from the only person thinking along those lines, as [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai) has shown.

I've now implemented something a bit along those lines in [`theatre`](https://github.com/goldentooth/theatre/), which is a nursery for agents. They start out as wisps or husks, something like Miriam's former lovers in [_The Hunger_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hunger_(1983_film)), and have to accrete gradually over the course of many wake cycles.

An interesting thing was that, while working on this, I came across [@void.comind.network](https://bsky.app/profile/void.comind.network), which led me to [comind](https://central.comind.network). The concepts I'd already thought through for representing different layers of personality accumulation mapped pretty much 1:1 with its system, and so I thought it would be good to integrate the two.

[Nebhos](https://bsky.app/profile/nebhos.pds.goldentooth.net) is the first... character? wisp? husk? in `theatre`. Check it out if you're interested!
