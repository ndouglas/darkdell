Dragonstone is a Rust library for the procedural generation of political systems and interactions in games. It provides tools and utilities for creating dynamic and immersive game worlds with rich and complex political landscapes. The library supports various aspects of political systems, including factions, diplomacy, and the ability to influence or change the political landscape of the game world.

Dragonstone is designed to be flexible and extensible, with a modular architecture that allows for easy integration with other game systems. It is intended for use by game developers who want to create complex and immersive game worlds with detailed political systems and interactions.
<!--more-->

## Motivations

The motivation behind Dragonstone is to provide game developers with a powerful and flexible tool for creating political systems and interactions in games. By leveraging procedural generation techniques, Dragonstone aims to provide a comprehensive solution for developers who want to create immersive and dynamic game worlds with rich political landscapes.

## Challenges

Some of the challenges that may be faced during the development of Dragonstone include:

- Designing a flexible and extensible architecture that can accommodate a wide range of political systems and mechanics
- Optimizing performance to ensure that the library can handle large-scale games with complex political systems
- Balancing the procedural generation of political systems with the need for player agency and meaningful choices
- Ensuring compatibility with other game systems and providing clear documentation and examples for integration
